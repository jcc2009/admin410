Financing

With the Durham House of Pets Credit Card, you can take advantage of our financing offers.

  1. Option 1-Balance PAID in full within 6 months. You will NOT be charged interest
  2. Option 2-Balance NOT paid in full within 6 months. You WILL be charged the regular interest rate from the date of purchase.

NOTE: interest rates will vary based on the period of time.

Delivery

Delivery cost are determined by the total weight of the order, location of the delivery and by the delivery method selected. We do offer FREE SHIPPING on orders totaling $100 and up.

Return Policies

We will gladly accept returns within 30 days of the purchase. If you do not have the receipt, you will be issued store credit. All clearance items are FINAL sale. We guarantee the lowest price on everything you buy. If you find a lower price, we will refund you the difference.
